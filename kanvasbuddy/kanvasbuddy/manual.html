KanvasBuddy

A floating minimalist GUI

Usage

Panel Buttons
The top row of buttons control which "panel" (docker/widget) is shown on KB.

Brush Preset Sliders
These sliders control various properties of the current brush preset. There's also a slider for rotating the canvas.

Canvas Options
The bottom row of buttons toggle different properties of the canvas, such as canvas-only mode, horizontal mirroring, and instant preview mode.

Configuring KanvasBuddy

You can change which dockers, sliders and buttons are featured on KB and the order they appear in by setting and moving the flags in config.ini.

Panel sizes and button icons are possible to edit by changing values in data.json. This is discouraged unless you're confident you know what you're doing, though.
